Lasagna

Description

Choose hot or sweet Italien sausage for this classic lasagna recipe. It takes some
time to make, but it feed a crowd, and the leftovers are even better the next day.

This recipe is comfort food at its finest, layered with ground beef, Italian sausage,
and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound bulk Italian sausage
  • 4 (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • 1 (16 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • ½ teaspoon dried thyme
  • ¼ teaspoon dried basil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • ⅓ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 pound shredded mozzarella cheese

    • Steps

    1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    2. Cook ground beef and sausage in a large pot over
      medium heat; drain. Stir in tomato sauce, mushrooms,
      garlic salt, oregano, thyme, and basil; season with
      salt and black pepper. Simmer 30 minutes.
    3. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water
      to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles with olive oil in the
      boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet
      firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain.
    4. Meanwhile, combine ricotta cheese, eggs, and Parmesan
      cheese in a bowl.
    5. Cover bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish with a thin
      layer of meat sauce; layer 3 lasagna noodles over top.
      Spread about 1/3 ricotta cheese mixture over noodles; sprinkle
      about 1/4 mozzarella cheese over top. Spoon about 1/3
      meat sauce over mozzarella cheese. Repeat layering twice
      more, finishing with remaining mozzarella cheese.
    6. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese melts and
      sauce bubbles, about 90 minutes. Cover lasagna with foil
      if cheese begins to get too dark. Let sit before serving
      for 10 to 15 minutes.
